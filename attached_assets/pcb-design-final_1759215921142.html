Design Process

Proven PCB Design Methodology

Our systematic 6-step process ensures optimal design outcomes, on-time delivery, and manufacturing success from requirements to production.

1

Requirements Analysis

Timeline: 1-2 days

Detailed consultation to understand your specifications, performance requirements, environmental constraints, and project objectives.

Deliverables:
  • Technical specification document
  • Project timeline and milestones
  • Component recommendations
2

Schematic Design

Timeline: 3-5 days

Complete circuit schematic creation with optimal component selection and comprehensive electrical rule checking.

Deliverables:
  • Complete schematic design
  • Bill of materials (BOM)
  • Design rule check (DRC) report
3

PCB Layout

Timeline: 5-10 days

Precision PCB layout with optimized component placement, intelligent routing, and comprehensive signal integrity analysis.

Deliverables:
  • Complete PCB layout files
  • 3D visualization and renderings
  • Layer stackup documentation
4

Design Verification

Timeline: 2-3 days

Comprehensive verification including design rule checks, electrical testing, thermal analysis, and signal integrity simulation.

Deliverables:
  • Verification and test reports
  • Simulation results and analysis
  • Manufacturing readiness assessment
5

Documentation

Timeline: 1-2 days

Complete manufacturing package preparation with assembly drawings, fabrication notes, and comprehensive specifications.

Deliverables:
  • Gerber files and drill data
  • Assembly drawings and specifications
  • Manufacturing and testing notes
6

Production Support

Timeline: Ongoing

Continuous support during manufacturing and assembly phases to ensure successful production and quality outcomes.

Support includes:
  • Manufacturing vendor support
  • Assembly guidance and troubleshooting
  • Testing protocols and validation
